About TimmyGram

Short videos for children, curated by the people who know them best.

TimmyGram is built for parents who want a simple way to share meaningful videos with their children without sending them into a public social media environment.

Why TimmyGram exists

Children love watching familiar faces, silly moments, songs, stories, pets, family greetings, and little memories. TimmyGram gives parents a dedicated place to collect those videos and make them easy for children to watch.

Instead of public feeds, comments, recommendations, or distractions, TimmyGram focuses on parent-approved content and a child-friendly viewing experience.

Built around families

  • Parent-managed uploads
  • Simple short-video viewing
  • Designed for children’s devices
  • Focused on private family moments
